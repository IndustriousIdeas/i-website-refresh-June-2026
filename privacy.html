Privacy & cookies

What this site collects, and why.

Plain-language notes on cookies, the contact form, and the tools behind them. Last updated July 1, 2026.

Cookies

This site uses cookies, including Klaviyo’s onsite tracking script, which sets a cookie to recognize returning visitors and helps us understand how people use the site. By continuing to use this site, you are agreeing to this use of cookies. Your browser’s local storage is also used to remember that you’ve seen the cookie notice, so it doesn’t reappear on every visit.

The contact form and email

Submitting the contact form is separate from cookie consent. When you submit it, your name, email, and message are sent to Klaviyo so we can respond to you — this happens regardless of your cookie choice, because it’s the direct result of an action you took.

If you check “Keep me posted on Industrious Ideas,” you are also added to our email list in Klaviyo. This is the only way you’re added to marketing email — it is never triggered by cookie consent, and cookie consent never adds you to it. Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe link.

Who processes this data

Klaviyo (email and onsite analytics) — see Klaviyo’s privacy policy.

Vercel (site hosting) — see Vercel’s privacy policy.

Your choices

You can review this notice again anytime via the “Cookie notice” link in the footer of any page. Unsubscribe from marketing email using the link in any email you receive from us, or by emailing the address below.

Questions
